About me

What was my path to UX?

I started my career as a print and digital designer for a research agency. It was my job to take survey data and present it in clear and informative reports. This research would then guide top UK companies to make user-focused improvements to their websites.

I realised that my love of design and tech, together with this research background, could come together in a job as a UX Designer.

So I dived into a year-long intensive bootcamp in User Experience and User Interface Design.
