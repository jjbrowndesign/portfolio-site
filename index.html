About me

What was my path to UX?

I started my career as a print and digital designer for a research agency. We conducted research to guide top UK companies to make user-led improvements to their websites.

I realised that my eye for design and love of tech, together with this research background, could come together in a job as a UX Designer.

So I took a year-long intensive bootcamp in UX and UI Design, covering web and app design plus frontend development (HTML and CSS).
